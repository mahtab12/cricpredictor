Access & Reimbursement

Key Considerations

US

  • Medicare Advantage plans provide less favorable coverage than commercial plans.
  • Emerging therapies obtain low cost-sharing through discounted price.
  • HEOR does not always secure preferred plan coverage.

EU5

  • HTA is the firmly established P&R gatekeeper for T2D therapies.
  • Level of benefit/value/innovation drives P&R terms.
  • However, the use of HbA1c to determine efficacy is unlikely to result in additional benefit.
